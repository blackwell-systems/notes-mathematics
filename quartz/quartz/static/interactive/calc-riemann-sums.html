The shaded area is the approximation for the current n and method. As n → ∞ the sum converges to the exact integral: the error → 0. Midpoint and Trapezoidal converge much faster (error ~ 1/n2) than Left/Right (error ~ 1/n); Simpson is faster still (~ 1/n4).
