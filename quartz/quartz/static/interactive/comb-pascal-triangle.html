Pascal's Triangle Explorer

Each cell is a binomial coefficient C(n,k) ("n choose k"), the number of ways to choose k items from n. Hover or tap a cell to see Pascal's rule: every cell equals the sum of the two above it.

Compute C(n,r) = n! / (r! (n-r)!)

Symmetry: C(n,r) = C(n, n-r), because choosing which r to take is the same as choosing which n-r to leave out. Click a triangle cell to load its n and r here.
